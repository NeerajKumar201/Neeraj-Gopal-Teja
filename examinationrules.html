ONLINE EXAMINATION INSTRUTIONS

Steps to follow:

  1. Click on the Click here to open the exam link provided in the email.
  2. Click "Log In For Your Exam Here" at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Have your Proctor enter the Username and Password provided in the email and click enter.
  4. To begin the exam, click on the link to the appropriate exam listed under Online Assessments
  5. Then read the instructions carefully, and then attempt the questions accordingily.

During the exam:

  1. Students must complete the multiple-choice exam within the time frame allotted for the exam.
  2. Students must not stop the session and then return to it. This is especially important in the online environment where the system will "time-out" and not allow the student or you to reenter the exam site.

What to do if your online exam is interrupted

  1. If your online exam is interrupted, click the “Back” button on your web browser to see if you can return to the exam. If not, follow the instructions below to resume taking the exam.
  2. Reconnect to the Internet and log back into Blackboard.
  3. Follow your original instructions to access the exam login page.
  4. Ask your proctor to re-enter the Username and Password.
  5. Have your Proctor enter the Username and Password provided in the email and click enter.
